<:Title> VoltMiniConf <:Body>

Learn About Volt & Opal

Join the Volt and Opal community for an evening mini conference, including a few talks, free food and beer - immediately following RubyConf's 2nd day. All Rubyists are welcome and encouraged to come whether you are an experienced or new to Volt and Opal. Bring a friend!

Remote or in Person

If you are going to be at RubyConf in San Antonio, we will be renting a conference room near Rubyconf. (Location is TBA). If you can't make it, we will be streaming all talks and taking questions from the stream.

Speakers


Ryan Stout

Ryan is the creator of volt. He will be discussing upcoming features of Volt, common patterns, and growing the community.

Rick Carlino

Rick will give a quick intro to Volt for those who are new. Rick runs the DataMelon screencast series.
