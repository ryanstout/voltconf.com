<:Title> VoltMiniConf <:Body>
What is it?

The Volt and Opal communities are doing an evening miniconference during RubyConf. The evening of Monday the 16th, we invite you to join other people interested in Volt/Opal for a few talks, community, free food and beer.

In Person and Remote

If you are going to be at RubyConf in San Antonio, we will be renting a conference room near Rubyconf. (Location is TBA). If you can't make it, we will be streaming all talks and taking questions from the stream.

Speakers

Ryan Stout

Ryan is the creator of volt. He will be discussing upcoming features of Volt, common patterns, and growing the community.

Elia

Elia is one of the lead developers of Opal. Elia will dive into some of the way Opal maintains performance without breaking Ruby compatibility.
